Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

1. Research and analyze keywords.

2. Improve on-page components such as content, organization, and meta tags.

3. Build high-quality backlinks and boost domain authority.

4. Conduct technical audits for SEO.

Social Media Marketing(SMM)

1. Create and maintain profiles on networks including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and TikTok.

2. Create calendars of interesting material.

3. Execute focused advertising efforts and track engagement indicators.

4. Foster and cultivate relationships within the community.

Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

1. Oversee pay-per-click (PPC) advertising campaigns on Bing Ads and Google Ads.

2. Use audience targeting and A/B testing to maximize ad success.

3. Offer ROI reporting and analytics.

Analytics and Reporting

1. To monitor performance, use tools like SEMrush, HubSpot, and Google Analytics.

2. Offer thorough reports with insights that may be put to use.

3. Recognize patterns and suggest tactical modifications.
